The year is 6,000 AD and mankind has just emerged from another Dark Ages period.

Will you help mankind rise again or plunge them into the enternal abyss?

Features:

* The most extensive tech tree of any MOD with 450+ technologies that will require many playthroughs to fully explroe rich trees in 1) Medicine & Biology, 2) Physics & Energy, 3) Pschosocial Engineering, and 4) Applied Technology
* Massive unit variants that allow playing from many different tactical strategies with over 1800 units
* Take your House from WW1-esque technology to the advent of the 4th Republic

Reimagined for the latest version of EFS.

Current version is 0.4A - this MOD is still in development, but very playable.

Please visit https://github.com/Matt-Caspermeyer/Hyperion/blob/main/README.md for more information as I continue developing Hyperion to its ultimate and final version and provide feedback on your playing experience.
