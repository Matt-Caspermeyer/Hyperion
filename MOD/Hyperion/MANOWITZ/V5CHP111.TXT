Occultists / Visioneers are specialized units designated with a (Psy7) after the unit's name. These types of units are the next level upgrade to the Vis Craft Soldiers (Psy6) and offer a slight increase to their statistics across the board compared to their previous level.

Note that the upgrades are done at a Church.


Occultists - Allows the fielding of Vislocks better connected to the Holy Flame, increasing their operational efficiency.
