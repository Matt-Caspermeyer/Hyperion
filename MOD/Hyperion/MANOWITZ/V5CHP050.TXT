Cybernetically-Enhanced Soldiers are specialized units designated with a (CE) after the unit's name. These types of units are the next upgrade to Prosthetically-Augmented Soldiers (PA) and offer improvements across the board.

The unit is enhanced via the use of cybernetics and nano implants and the upgrade procedure is performed at a Lab to ensure its success.
