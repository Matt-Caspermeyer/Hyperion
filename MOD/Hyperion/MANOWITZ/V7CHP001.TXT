The Foot movement type is one of the most versatile terrestrial movement types.

It is comprised mainly of foot soldiers or other types that mainly use their legs for locomotion.

They are capable of moving on any land terrain hex and typically suffer reduced penalities when moving through rough terrain.


The following lists the movement class terrain agility bonus or penalty additive values (all others have no effect):

Ice		-1
Mountain	+3
Hill		+2
Tree		+6
River		-1
