Machine-Fused Soldiers are specialized units designated with an (MF) after the unit's name. These types of units are the next upgrade to Cybernetically-Enhanced Soldiers (CE) and offer improvements across the board, but especially in agility and accuracy.

The unit is enhanced via the use of Wetware and the procedure is performed at a Wetware Plant to ensure its success.
