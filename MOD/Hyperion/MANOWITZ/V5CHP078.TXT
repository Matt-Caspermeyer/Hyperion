Sympathic Soldiers are specialized units designated with a (Psy4) after the unit's name. These types of units are the next level upgrade to the Telepathic Soldiers (Psy3) and offer a slight increase to their statistics across the board compared to their previous level.

Note that the upgrades are done at a Church.
