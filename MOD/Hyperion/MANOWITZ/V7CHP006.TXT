The Space movement type is able to transit between the ground and space.

It is comprised mainly of mechanical units that use powerful rocket engines to transit between land and space.

They cannot use the jump gates, but move on the ground just like Air units, unencumbered by any terrestrial terrain.

Note that they can suffer damage when landing on the ground outside of cities.


The following lists the weapons that can target this movement class:

Ranged Space
Direct Space
Close Space
