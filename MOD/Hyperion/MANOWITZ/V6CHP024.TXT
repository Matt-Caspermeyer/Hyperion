A Lab is one of a ruler's most important cities.

Labs are responsible for conducting all research and progress cannot be made or maintained without one.

Note that the more technologies a Lab contains, the more research points that are going towards technology maintenance rather than technology research.

An astute ruler will build more Labs, as needed, to ensure that most of the Lab's research points go towards the development of new technologies rather than the maintenace of old ones.

If technology becomes too old or obsolete, a ruler may decide to strike (or remove) technologies no longer required or needed to reduce the amount of points that are being used for technology maintenance.
