Surgically-Enhanced Soldiers are specialized units designated with an (SE) after the unit's name. These types of units are an upgrade to their base unit and often offer a substantial increase to their base statistics compared to their base unit.

The unit undergoes several surgical procedures at a Hospital which may include various implants or prosthetics to improve most of their base statistics across the board.
