Antivirally-Enhanced Soldiers are specialized units designated with an (AVE) after the unit's name. These types of units are the next upgrade to Genetically-Enhanced Soldiers (GE) and often offer a notable increase in most unit statistics.

Note that there are several technological improvements compared to Genetically-Enhanced Soldiers on the genetic level granting these units immunity to the Plague as well as additional soldier performance.

All unit upgrades are performed at a Lab to ensure that the procedure is successful.
